ABOUT US

By offering an honest diagnosis of your cars problem as well with highly trained staff, Burke Tires & Auto Repair prevent unnecessary work being done to your vehicle. We appreciate that what you want is a trustworthy, efficient service, and so by isolating the essence of the issue prior to carrying out any work, we can save you money and ensure your vehicle repairs are done right the first time. The sooner we isolate the issue, the sooner we can get you back driving safely.
